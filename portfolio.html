30 Days 30 Sites

I have started a challenge of creating sites within 30 days. The only way to become a better developer is by creating sites and trying out new elements and different things. With this challenge, there are a great variety of sites to complete, which will help in develop skills to become a better developer.

Although most of these sites took more than a day, I think the time that I took is good for starting out. I used to plan out the site and code it all between a week or two. Now I gotten to a 3 day restriction. I wanted to take my time with these sites because this is the first project that I have taken on.
